St. Basil's Morth Mariam Samajam

(St. Mary's Women's League)

Our desire is to deepen our spiritual lives by learning and understanding God’s Word and applying it to our personal lives.


The activities of Morth Mariam Samajam compliments the overall functioning of the church and its various spiritual bodies. Samajam members meet every first/second Sunday at church after the holy mass to read the bible, sing devotional songs and pray for the needy.

We organize annual lenten retreats, food sale and various fund raisers and the fund collected is handed out to the needy.

“Favour is deceitful, and beauty is vain: but the woman that feareth the Lord, shall be praised” (Proverbs 31:30)

Please join us in prayer for the continuing guidance of the Holy Spirit in all of our activities.
