Prayers & Worships

St. Basil's Prayer Meeting

As a way to enrich our faith, we organize prayer meetings at church attendee’s houses every month. We use this opportunity to pray together, listen/participate in enlightening discussion on faith and religion. This also has been a great opportunity to mold our upcoming young generation around faith. Young children says prayers, sing christian songs, and tell biblical stories during this event – which also has been playing key role in personal development of kids.

Prayer Request

For those who cannot be present during the prayers at St Basil’s Church, you can submit your intercessory prayer request to St. Mor Baseliose Eldho Bava or St. Mary using the following request form. Alternating every week, intercessory prayer to St. Mor Baseliose Eldho Bava and St. Mary will be held during Holy Qurbono in our church. You can pick the date for your prayer request on the scheduler by just clicking on the date. If the date you picked happens to be a date with no Holy Qurbono or intercessory prayer you requested (to St. Mor Baseliose Eldho Bava or to St. Mary) is not scheduled for that week , your prayer request will be held in the following Holy Qurbono. Please submit your request at least two days before your date of request.
